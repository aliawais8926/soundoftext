How to Use?


Using our sound of text tool to generate the voices in different languages with the voice of male and female is very easy. You can follow the simple steps.

  • Enter your text or Paste a maximum of 200 words into the input container.
  • Select the Language or Voice.
  • Click on the Generate button, the tool will start generating the voice for you
  • As soon the voice is generated a voice player will show up. You can play the sound to check if it is according to your needs or not.
  • You can download the voice by clicking on the download button
